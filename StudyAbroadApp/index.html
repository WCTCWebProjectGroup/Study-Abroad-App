In each bag, place a copy of Itinerary with dates and addresses/phone numbers as well as Home address and phone numbers (in case bag is separated from you).

Ideally, 1 checked bag with wheels and 1 brief-case – everything you take will have to be carried everywhere you go, including on subways, busses, trams, etc.

Good idea to have a change of clothes with your carry-on in case your luggage does not make it the first day.

Electrical connectors/plug adaptors must be country specific. Check electrical outlets for all countries you will be in. It is most probable all electrical devices you take will be 120/240v and you will not need a converter.

Zip-lock bags of various sizes very helpful to keep things organized in briefcase and suitcase. Red Sendiks bags are also helpful to have in suitcase.

After final security, buy a bottle of water for each flight.
